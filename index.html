DEGREE OFFERS

We offer degree for persons who want to obtain a Undergraduate and Postgraduate Degrees.

Undergraduate

Are the first level of study, and they typically take three or four years to complete. They provide a broad foundation of knowledge in a particular field, and they can also help students develop critical thinking, communication, and research skills. Some common undergraduate degrees include Bachelor of Arts (BA), Bachelor of Science (BS), and Bachelor of Engineering (BE).

Postgraduate

Are more advanced degrees that students can pursue after they have completed an undergraduate degree. They typically take one to two years to complete, and they focus on a specific area of study. Postgraduate degrees can help students develop a deeper understanding of their chosen field, and they can also qualify them for certain professions. Some common postgraduate degrees include Master of Arts (MA), Master of Science (MSc), and Doctor of Philosophy (PhD).
